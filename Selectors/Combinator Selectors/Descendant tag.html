Descendant tag

Means that the tag is directly or in-directly related to that given tag

Directly related to div tag child of div tag

In-directly related to div tag and child of p tag

Child of h2 tag and in directly related to div and p tag's

        Syntax:

        parent_tag  child_tag 
        
        {
            property1:value1;
            property2:value2;

        }

        Example:

        div  p
        
        {
            color:green;
            font-size:36px;
        }
