Group Selectors

This Group Selector are used to give same style to many tag at a time by separate that tag's by using , (comma) 

        Syntax:

        tag_1 , tag_2 , tag_3 .... 

        {
            property1:value1;
            property2:value2;
            .........
        }

        Example:

        h1 , p , h4 {
            color:green;
            font-size:36px;
        }
